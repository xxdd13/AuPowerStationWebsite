Observations

Australia uses a huge variety of power generators.
Steam Type Generator dominates Australian Power Generation.
New South Wales has the highest power generation (22,000 MWatts),
while Australian Capital Territory has the least (less than 1,000 MWatts).

The number of power stations that uses natural gas as primary fuel is the highest of all, with 29.26% of total fuels
However, this does not indicates the amount of energy natural gas can generate

Coal is the most common fuel type used to generate power, followed by natural gas.
This makes sense as Australia is rich in both coal and natural gas deposits.
However, this poses a problem as these two fuel types are non-renewable and there doesn't appear to be viable replacement to date


None-Renewable source is the most popular than Renewable source.
However, most companies uses both renewable and non-renewable source.
An increase in renewable usage is expected in the future
